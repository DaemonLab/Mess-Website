Rules And Policies

  1. Any act of manhandling, group fighting, intimidation or violence, wilful damage to property or drunken behaviour will be considered as a serious offence. Strict punitive action will be taken against person/s involved in such activities.
  2. The students shall not indulge in any immoral act and misbehaviour with any staff/service provider, dining hall staff, dining committee members, hospitality manager, hall of residence manager, caretaker, etc.,. It will lead to strict disciplinary action. Your conduct and language should reflect the reputation of the Institute.
  3. Meals will be served strictly during indicated timings of operation only.
  4. In case of illness, sick diet will be provided to the students upon receipt of a written advice from the Institute Medical Officer. Hall residents are advised to inform the caterer in advance about the requirements of any special meal.
  5. Hall residents should not take the utensils/food outside the dining hall. (Exempted only under circumstances mentioned in rule no. 3).
  6. No hall resident is allowed to enter the kitchen area.
  7. No dinner is allowed to share his/her food catered in dining facility with others under any circumstances.
  8. Only those Suggestions/Complaints which are submitted through the Hall of Residence Portal will be considered. The language used for such purpose should be strictly official and the complaint should be objective, rather than emotive.
  9. Students are advised to approach appropriate authority and are strictly prohibited to use social media/press as platform to address their grievance.
  10. The institute strictly prohibits the unlawful possession, use, or distribution of illicit drugs and alcohol by students in the campus and it reserves the right to take the strict action, expulsion from Hall of Residence to expulsion from the institute.

(a)Noncompliance of rules 1-10 may lead to disciplinary action decided by Wardens/Hall of Residence staff.
(b) The decision regarding fines and penalties imposed is non-negotiable; hence no discussion/ argument will be entertained.

POLICY FOR AVAILING REBATE IN DINING FACILITY

1. Short Duration Rebate (Between 2-7 days)
The short duration rebate form can be availed in case of short term absence of a student with a minimum number of 2 days and maximum duration of upto 7 days. Students can avail this short duration rebate by filling the following google form circulated by the Students Dining Committee.
Link for Google Form: https://forms.gle/Y8RM86LKorQt4RNx8

Policy of Rebate for Students:
  1. A student can avail rebate for a minimum of 2 days with maximum period of up-to 7 days.
  2. A maximum of 8 days rebate per month in total will be provided through the short term rebate forms.
  3. Students need to fill the form 2 days prior up-to 5 pm before the leave date after that no application will be accepted.
  4. There is no requirement of faculty supervisor/HOD signatures for availing short duration rebate.
Circulation: The google form will be circulated on institute mail by the Students Dining Committee explaining the policy and how to avail it. Also, QR codes (Containing link of same google form) will be pasted on the notice boards in the Central Dining Facility. Students can write an email or contact Students Dining Committee members in case any assistance is required. The students after filling out the rebate form will get a system generated copy of their form for reference.

Information to the Caterer: The Students Dining Committee will cross check the data of the particular day and check for any discrepancies in the filled form. Valid and approved rebate form data will be provided to the caterer by 10 pm each day via email keeping the Dining Warden and Dining Warden office in Cc.

Note: This will be done by the Content writing team of the Students Dining Committee

Members: Ishaan Mittal (Ishaan Mittal), Arunaditya Dash(Arunaditya Dash),Jugal Shah(Jugal Shah), Aditya Jadhav (Aditya Jadhav).

Billing
The calculation of the rebate of a student will be done by the Dining warden office. Rebate details will be displayed to students in the form of email or website or on notice boards, thereafter payments will be disbursed to the caterers.

2. Long Duration Rebate (Greater than 7 days)
  1. Long duration mess rebate claims will be considered for academic reasons (attending a seminar, workshop, conference, internship, etc.,), Department-sanctioned leave (casual leave, ODL, etc.,), and medical reasons
  2. Students can avail long duration rebate by filling a hardcopy form present on the website and submitting it along with supporting documents (i.e. Approved leave letter from your department signed by supervisor/faculty advisor/ HoD or an official order from academic section or Medical certificate (in the case of under medical grounds) to the respective hostel office 2 days before the leave date.
  3. Dining rebate will be approved only when the above rules are followed.
